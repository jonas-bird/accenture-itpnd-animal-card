American Black Bear

The American black bear is the most widespread bear species in North America.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus americanus
  • Average Weight: 125-600lbs
  • Habitat: 1.6-1.75 meters
  • Typical Diet: Forests, wooded mountains, and swamps

It is estimated that there are between 767,000 to 914,000 black bears in North America. Despite the name they can be other colors such as reddish brown, cream, and even a gray blue!
